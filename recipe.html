Peperoni Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange sliced pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  6. Brush the edges of the dough with olive oil if desired.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Slice and enjoy your delicious pepperoni pizza!
